On-Chain Metrics and Tokenomics of Metal

Understanding Metal’s tokenomics and on-chain activity is essential for anyone analyzing the metal cryptocurrency price. Tokenomics explains how the supply and demand of MTL tokens work, while on-chain metrics show how active the network is. Together, these two elements help predict potential price movements in 2025.

Metal has a limited total supply of tokens, which creates scarcity over time. This fixed cap means that as adoption grows, the available supply on exchanges becomes smaller, supporting the price. However, when locked tokens are released from staking or vesting programs, temporary price dips can occur.

Main Tokenomics Factors:

  • Total Supply: A limited supply helps prevent long-term inflation.
  • Circulating Supply: The amount of Metal currently traded on exchanges influences liquidity and volatility.

On-chain data also provides insight into network health. When the number of active addresses, daily transactions, and total value locked (TVL) grows, it signals a stronger ecosystem. For Metal, these increases often precede a rise in market confidence.
