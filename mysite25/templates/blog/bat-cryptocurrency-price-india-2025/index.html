What is BAT and How It Works

The Basic Attention Token (BAT) was created to change how digital advertising works. It is the native token of the Brave Browser, a privacy-focused browser that blocks unwanted ads and trackers. Instead of giving personal data to advertisers, users can choose to view ads and get paid in BAT. This system makes advertising more transparent and fair.

Here’s how the Brave ecosystem functions:

  • Users – They browse the internet with privacy and earn BAT by watching ads they choose to see.
  • Advertisers – They pay for campaigns using BAT and reach real, verified users who are interested in their content.
  • Publishers and creators – They receive BAT tips and rewards directly from users and advertisers for quality content.

This circular model ensures that everyone in the system benefits. The demand for the token increases as more users and advertisers join the Brave platform. With over 60 million monthly active users worldwide and a growing base in India, this creates a steady use case for BAT in the India cryptocurrency market.

BAT also operates on the Ethereum blockchain, ensuring transparency and security. It can be traded or stored like other cryptocurrencies.
