Privacy Policy

Last updated: October 16, 2025

At CryptoBharat, we value your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, share, and safeguard your information in accordance with Indian data protection regulations, including the Information Technology (Reasonable Security Practices and Procedures and Sensitive Personal Data or Information) Rules, 2011 and the Digital Personal Data Protection Act, 2023.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Information You Provide Voluntarily

When you use our site, you may provide us with the following information:

  • Full name
  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • Messages sent through our contact form
  • Comments or contributions on our articles

1.2 Information Collected Automatically

We automatically collect certain technical data during your visit:

  • IP address
  • Browser type and operating system
  • Pages visited and visit duration
  • Approximate location data (city, state)
  • Cookies and similar technologies

2. How We Use Your Information

Your personal data is used to:

  • Respond to inquiries and support requests
  • Improve your experience on our website
  • Send newsletters (with your prior consent)
  • Analyze trends and usage statistics
  • Ensure security and prevent misuse
  • Comply with legal and regulatory obligations

3. Sharing Your Information

CryptoBharat never sells your personal data. We may share your information only in the following situations:

  • Service Providers: trusted partners (hosting, analytics, mailing) who process data on our behalf
  • Legal Obligations: when required by law or to protect our legal rights
  • Explicit Consent: when you have expressly allowed us to share your data

4. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

Our site uses cookies to enhance your browsing experience. Cookies are small files stored on your device. You can manage or disable cookies in your browser settings.

Types of cookies used:

  • Essential Cookies: necessary for the website to function properly
  • Analytics Cookies: to analyze site performance and user behavior (Google Analytics)
  • Preference Cookies: to remember your settings (language, dark/light mode)

5. Your Rights

Under Indian data protection laws, you have the following rights:

  • Right of Access: view the data we hold about you
  • Right of Rectification: correct inaccurate or outdated information
  • Right of Erasure: request deletion of your personal data
  • Right to Object: refuse certain data processing activities
  • Right to Data Portability: receive your data in a structured format
  • Withdrawal of Consent: withdraw consent at any time

To exercise your rights, contact us at: contact@cryptobharat-in.com

6. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your data from unauthorized access, loss, or disclosure:

  • SSL/TLS encryption for all data transmissions
  • Restricted access to personal data
  • Regular system monitoring
  • Frequent security updates

7. Data Retention

We retain your personal data only as long as necessary for the purposes for which it was collected or as required by law:

  • Contact data: while you are subscribed to our newsletter
  • Analytics data: up to 26 months
  • Communication data: 2 years after last interaction

8. External Links

Our site may contain links to third-party websites (crypto platforms, social networks). We are not responsible for their privacy practices. Please review their privacy policies before sharing your information.

9. Protection of Minors

Our site is not intended for individuals under 18 years old. We do not knowingly collect data from minors. If you believe we have done so inadvertently, please contact us immediately.

10. Changes to This Policy

We reserve the right to update this Privacy Policy at any time. Updates will take effect once published on this page. Please review this page regularly for changes.

11. Jurisdiction

This Privacy Policy is governed by the laws of India. Any dispute will be subject to the exclusive jurisdiction of the courts of Bengaluru, Karnataka.
