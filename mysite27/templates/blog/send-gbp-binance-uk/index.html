How to Send GBP to Binance from the UK: Step-by-Step Guide

Before transferring your funds, you’ll need a verified Binance account. The process is straightforward and ensures that your account is secure and ready for transactions. Below are the steps to set up your account and send GBP safely from the UK.

Step 1: Create Your Binance Account

  • Visit the Binance Website: Go to the official Binance homepage and click the ‘Register’ button. The registration process is quick and simple, taking only a few minutes.
  • Provide Your Details: Enter your email address and create a strong password that includes letters, numbers, and special characters for maximum security.

Step 2: Complete Identity Verification (KYC)

Why Identity Verification Is Necessary: Binance requires all users to complete Know Your Customer (KYC) verification. This step ensures compliance with international financial regulations and protects your account from fraud.

  • Upload Documents: Follow the on-screen prompts to upload a valid ID (passport, driver’s licence, or national ID) along with any additional documents required.
  • Verification Approval: The review process may take a few days. Once approved, you’ll receive a confirmation email allowing you to access all Binance features.

Step 3: Enhance Your Account Security

  • Enable Two-Factor Authentication (2FA): Add an extra layer of protection by enabling 2FA. You can use apps like Google Authenticator or Authy for secure login verification.
  • Set Up an Anti-Phishing Code: Create a personal code that Binance will include in all official emails to help you recognise genuine messages.

Methods to Send GBP to Binance

Once your account is set up and verified, you can transfer GBP to Binance using several methods. The most common and cost-effective option for UK users is a bank transfer. This method typically offers low fees and fast processing times.

Sending GBP via Bank Transfer

  • Log Into Your Binance Account: Sign in using your verified credentials and ensure your account is ready for deposits.
  • Go to the ‘Deposit’ Section: Click on ‘Wallet’ → ‘Fiat and Spot’, then select ‘Deposit’ to start the process.
  • Select GBP as Your Currency: From the currency list, choose GBP to ensure that your funds are correctly deposited.
  • Choose Deposit Method: Select ‘Bank Transfer’ (Faster Payments) as your deposit option. This is often the most reliable and affordable choice for UK residents.
  • Follow the Instructions: Binance will display the necessary banking details, including account number, sort code, and reference code. Use these details to send GBP from your UK bank account. Double-check all information to prevent delays or errors.

Tip: Transfers made through UK bank accounts using the Faster Payments Service (FPS) usually arrive within minutes, although some may take up to a few hours depending on your bank.
