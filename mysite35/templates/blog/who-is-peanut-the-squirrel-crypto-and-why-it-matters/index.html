History of Peanut the Squirrel: From Living Creature to Meme Coin

The story behind Peanut the Squirrel is what sets this crypto project apart from many others. Peanut was not just a digital idea but a real eastern gray squirrel rescued by Mark Longo in 2017. Mark saved Peanut after finding him injured and cared for him for several years. Their bond grew strong, and Peanut became a beloved companion.

However, in 2024, the story took a sad turn. Authorities in New York State confiscated Peanut and later euthanized him. This event caused a lot of public outcry and sparked debates about animal rights and government authority. Many people were upset by how the situation was handled, and Peanut quickly became a symbol of resistance against unfair regulations.

Inspired by this emotional and social story, a group of developers created the Peanut the Squirrel crypto token, known as PNUT.
